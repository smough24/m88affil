Affiliate Program
What is M88 Affiliates Program?
The M88 Affiliates Program is a partnership program where you can earn commissions based on the net loss of wagering activity of players that you refer to M88.com from your Website(s) via online or offline via referrals.
How can I register with the affiliate program?
There is no cost needed for you to join M88 Affiliate Program. Simply fill out our application form and you will be contacted within 72 hours (3 business days) regarding your acceptance into the program. If your application is approved, you will receive an email containing your unique affiliate ID, giving you access to banners, text links, and other content and marketing tools to add to your website(s).
What if I don't have a Website, can I still join?
To join in M88 Affiliates Program, you should have a Website (Live) that has online gambling as its central theme. If your Website does not have online gambling as its central theme or if don't have a website, some exceptions may be made at the sole discretion of M88.com. If you have other promoting resources, you are encouraged to join us as well. Please drop us an email at Enaffiliate@m88.com if you have any question.
What if I have more than one website?
If you have multiple websites, you only need to apply once to apply the M88 Affiliate Program and use the same affiliate ID for all the sites you own. You can also set up separate tracking codes for each site, so you can compare how well each site performs.
What are my responsibilities as an affiliate?
As an Affiliate, you are responsible for promoting M88 by implementing the advertising, banners and tracking URL's on your websites, e-mails or other communications.
Can an affiliate himself be a player as well?
Yes. The Affiliate himself can also be player; however, you cannot earn commissions on your own account. If you create a player account as a referral of yourself, your account will be removed from your referral portfolio.
Can I refer other affiliates? How?
Yes, you can refer other affiliates. Our Tier system allows you to earn from the sub-affiliate you refer to our Affiliate Program. To refer a sub-affiliate, all you need to do is to add his/her email address to the provided space in Sub-Affiliate section once you log into your Affiliate account. An Affiliate is not allowed to have more than one affiliate account.
